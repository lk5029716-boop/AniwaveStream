PLAYER UI
------------------------------------------------------------------------------

The in-app video player UI (Compose PlayerScreen + track selection helpers)
is original AniwaveStream work inspired by common streaming-app patterns
(Netflix / Crunchyroll style chrome). It is not a port of Dantotsu.

Playback engine: AndroidX Media3 ExoPlayer.
